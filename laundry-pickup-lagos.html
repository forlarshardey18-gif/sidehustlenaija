How to Start a Laundry & Dry Cleaning Pickup Service in Lagos (Low Capital Hustle)

Start from home with ₦100k–₦300k. Pickup & delivery model for busy Lagosians — earn ₦150k–₦500k/month.

Why Laundry Pickup is Booming in Lagos 2026

Busy professionals, families, hostels, hotels hate washing clothes. Pickup & delivery services save time — you pick up dirty clothes, wash/dry-clean, iron, and deliver fresh. Low competition in many estates → high repeat customers.

Startup Capital Breakdown (Low End)

Item Cost (₦)
Washing machine (used)₦80k–₦150k
Iron & board + detergents₦20k–₦40k
Delivery bike (used okada)₦100k–₦200k
Marketing (flyers, WhatsApp)₦10k–₦20k
Total startup₦210k–₦410k

Step-by-Step: Launch in 2–4 Weeks

  1. Week 1: Set Up Home Base
    Use backyard/space at home for washing/ironing. Buy used washing machine + industrial iron.
  2. Week 2: Get Equipment & Supplies
    Detergents, starch, hangers, delivery bags. Register business name with CAC (optional at start).
  3. Week 3: Marketing & First Customers
    Flyers in estates, WhatsApp status, Instagram page. Offer first wash free or discounted to neighbors.
  4. Week 4: Operations & Delivery
    Use bike for pickup/delivery. Charge ₦500–₦1,500 per kg/load. Build weekly/monthly plans.

Earnings Potential (Lagos 2026)

Scale Daily Loads Monthly Profit (after costs)
Starter (home-based)5–10₦150k–₦300k
Medium (hire 1 helper)15–25₦300k–₦500k
Scaled (2–3 staff + shop)30+₦500k–₦1M+

Pro Tips for Success in Lagos

  • Focus on estates/hotels — high volume, repeat customers.
  • Offer subscription plans (e.g. ₦10k/month for 50 items).
  • Use WhatsApp for bookings & updates — free marketing.
  • Partner with dry cleaning shops for special items (charge extra).
  • Start small — test with neighbors before buying more equipment.
