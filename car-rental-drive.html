Passive Income: How to Rent Out Your Car on Drive.ng & Make ₦150k+/month

Turn your idle car into cash flow — complete 2026 guide for car owners in Lagos, Abuja & other cities. Low effort, high return.

Why Renting Out Your Car is a Smart Passive Hustle in 2026

Many Nigerians own cars that sit idle most days. Platforms like Drive.ng, Autogirl, Moove, and others connect car owners with renters (tourists, expats, event planners, Uber/Bolt drivers). You can earn ₦100k–₦400k+/month with almost no daily work — just hand over keys and collect weekly/monthly payments.

How Drive.ng & Similar Platforms Work for Owners

  • Sign up as a car host (free) — upload car details, photos, documents (insurance, license, car papers).
  • Set your daily/weekly rate (e.g. ₦15k–₦40k/day depending on car type: Corolla, Camry, SUV, etc.).
  • Platform handles bookings, payments, basic verification of renters.
  • Most rentals include a driver (safer option) — you can choose self-drive for higher rates but more risk.
  • You get paid weekly/monthly via bank transfer after platform fee (10–20%).

Step-by-Step: Start Renting Your Car This Week

  1. Prepare Your Car: Valid insurance, roadworthy, clean interior/exterior. Get recent photos.
  2. Choose Platform: Drive.ng (peer-to-peer focus), Autogirl/Muvment (P2P with driver option), Moove (driver-focused but owner earnings possible).
  3. Sign Up & List: Upload documents, set price/availability. Approval takes 1–3 days.
  4. Meet Renters: Hand over car (with agreement/contract), take deposit if needed.
  5. Earn & Scale: Start with one car → add more as you get comfortable. Use earnings to buy second vehicle.

Pro Tips & Safety for Car Owners

  • Always use platform contracts + take photos of car condition before handover.
  • Require renter to have valid driver’s license + ID verification.
  • Choose "with driver" option for extra safety (platform often provides vetted drivers).
  • Track mileage/fuel usage — add clauses for excess wear/tear.
  • Start small: rent 2–3 days/week at first to test.
